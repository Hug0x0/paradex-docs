**Step 1:** Select a Vault on the Vaults page that you wish to sell tokens from

<Frame>
  <img src="../../../assets/vault-tokens-sell-1.png" alt="" />
</Frame>

**Step 2:** Click on the "Sell" button on the top right hand corner

<Frame>
  <img src="../../../assets/vault-tokens-sell-2.png" alt="" />
</Frame>

**Step 3:** Input the amount of vault tokens you with to sell and click "Sell"

<Frame>
  <img src="../../../assets/vault-tokens-sell-3.png" alt="" />
</Frame>

<p id="lockup">
  **Note:** You will not be able to sell your vault tokens if your minimum
  lockup period is not over yet
</p>
