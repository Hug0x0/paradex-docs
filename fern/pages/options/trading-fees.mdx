## Overview

The Taker pays **6bps of underlying notional** as a taker fee.

The Maker pays **1bp of underlying notional** as a maker fee.

Both Taker and Maker Fees are capped by 300% of the Option Price.

### Example 

Alice (taker) trades 2 BTC-USD-90000-C against Bob (maker). 
BTC spot price is 100000. 

Alice pays $$ 0.0006*2*100000=\$120 $$ and Bob pays $$ 0.0001*2*100000=\$20 $$
