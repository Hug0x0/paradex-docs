---
title: Trading Fees
---

Currently trading fees on Paradex are fixed and uniform across the platform.

| Instrument | Maker   | Taker |
| ---------- | ------- | ----- |
| Perpetual Futures | -0.005% | 0.03% |
| Perpetual Options | 0.03% | 0.03% |

<Note> Perpetual Options fees have a cap of 300% (3x) the Perpetual Option Price</Note>

**Notes**:

* Fees are collected on the notional value of the trade, which is the total value of the underlying assets being traded, irrespective of any leverage used.
* Currently fees are not subject to volume based discounts.
