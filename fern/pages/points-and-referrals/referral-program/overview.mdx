Paradex has officially launched its referral program! Key benefits include:

* You receive <span style={{color:'#C760ED'}}>**5%**</span> of referred user's fees paid
* You receive a points bonus of <span style={{color:'#C760ED'}}>**5%**</span> of referred user's points earned
* Your referrals get a <span style={{color:'#C760ED'}}>**5%**</span> discount on their fees
* You receive bonus XP points for your referrals activity

* Referred >$0 in lifetime volume
